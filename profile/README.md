## What we do

Todea is a Seoul-based engineering team. We help enterprises across the
Americas, EMEA, and APAC design, build, and operate production-grade
cloud-native infrastructure — and we stay on to run it.

We place senior cloud-native engineers inside your team to implement
platforms and keep them running: platform engineering, observability, cloud
migration, security and compliance, and 24/7 managed operations, delivered
by the same people who will operate the result. Engineers, not slide decks.

## What lives in this organization

This is where we open-source what generalizes cleanly out of our client work:

- **Reference architectures**: Kubernetes platforms, multi-cluster
  patterns, platform-engineering scaffolds.
- **Operators and controllers**: small, focused projects extracted from
  engagements.
- **Talks and workshops**: source material for what we present at KubeCon
  and regional Kubernetes Community Days.
- **Internal tooling worth sharing**: CLI helpers, linters, and
  conventions we maintain in public.

If a repo here doesn't have a clear README yet, it's early. Open an issue
and we'll prioritize it.

## How to engage

- **Hire engineers through us.** Embedded cloud-native engineers for
  platform builds, migrations, or day-two operations, on your team, under
  our contract. Reach out via [todea.co.kr](https://todea.co.kr).
- **File issues.** Bug reports and feature requests are welcome on any
  repo. Please include reproduction steps and the version you're running.
